Welcome to Bohol!

Bohol is a province in the Philippines situated in the Central Visayas region. It has a land area of 4,821 km2 (1,861 sq mi) and a coastline 261 km (162 mi) longÂ².

One of the towns in Bohol is Dimiao, which is located on the southern coast of the island. Dimiao has a population of 14,889 people as of 2020. Dimiao is known for its historical and natural attractions, such as the Ermita Ruins, the Punta Baluarte Eco-Museum, the Dimiao Twin Falls, and the Cambagat Cave. Dimiao is also famous for its weaving industry and its annual fiesta celebration.
